Our mission in the AIS is to raise awareness of AI and its potential to UTD and the surrounding communities. As a part of our K-12 outreach program, we hold AI workshops and give presentations to many high schools in Dallas and Houston.

Please check out our past and upcoming events below. If you are interested in having us speak or hold a workshop at your school, please feel free to get in touch with us at outreach@aisutd.org.
